What is Cartophoria?

Cartophoria is a concept storefront showcasing modern UI, smooth animations, product reviews and filtering — all made with vanilla HTML, CSS and JS, no frameworks.

Design System

Premium dark theme with a light variant, soft shadows, rounded corners, and subtle motion.

Performance

No build tools. Minimal assets. Everything loads fast and runs offline (icons/fonts require internet).
